Daslymz originated in Denver, Colorado in a small basement full of spiders. Two silly boys and their cranky dad decided to fend off boredom by creating a band out of songs their dad made up on the fly to tease them about their various shortcomings like being a fusspot, sulking, being slow in the morning, having smelly toes, etc. The name came from one of their frequent obsessions with making slime from glue and a bunch of other weird ingredients...
